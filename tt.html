Textile Technology

Textile technology is a program of study that can cover a broad range of topics, from chemistry and physics in textile production to textiles used in apparel and interior design. Keep reading to discover education and career options in this field.Textile technology programs teach the science of creating and enhancing fibers, fabrics and other textiles used in manufacturing, apparel, healthcare and consumer products. Training in textile technology prepares students for a number of different career paths, such as textile engineer, interior designer, materials scientist and researcher.
